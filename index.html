August 16, 2014 Poulsbo, WA

Together with our families we invite you to celebrate our wedding!

Saturday August 16th, 2014 from 2:30 - 9:00 pm

We invite you to spend the day with us. The event will last until 9:00 pm. You can choose to book overnight accommodations in the surrounding area or take the return ferry back to Seattle. The Bainbridge ferry runs as late as 1:00 am.

Where

Held on the rustic, northwest Red Cedar Farm in Poulsbo, WA.

27054 Lofall Road NW
Poulsbo, WA 98370

Driving

There is plenty of parking at the farm for anyone planning on driving to the wedding. Keep in mind, ferry lines will be a long wait due to summer travelers. Arrive at the ferry well before your intended departure time.

Shuttle

There will be 1 shuttle bringing guests to the ceremony. There will be 2 shuttles taking guests from the ceremony. The shuttle is run through Agate Pass Transportation. Each shuttle can carry up to 24 guests. We encourage you to take advantage of this service if you plan on drinking or just prefer the convinience.

Arrival Shuttle

  • 1:45 pm - Departing Bainbridge Island Ferry (1:10 pm ferry departing from Seattle)
  • 2:15 pm - Departing Clearwater Casino
  • 2:30 pm - Departing Guest House Inn and Suites
  • 2:50 pm - Arrive at Red Cedar Farm

Return Shuttle

  • 7:00 pm - Departing for Guest House Inn and Suites, Clearwater Casino and 8:10 pm Bainbridge ferry
  • 9:00 pm - Departing for Guest House Inn and Suites, Clearwater Casino and 10:30 pm Bainbridge ferry
